NotifyrFirmware

This repository is the home for Notifyr's awesome firmware. Running on a STM32L152 microcontroller, it processes commands sent over a BLE radio, and displays incoming notifications on a Sharp Memory LCD display.

To Modify and stuff, open in CoIDE, you'll need the STLink drivers to program the board, and ARM-GCC to compile.
